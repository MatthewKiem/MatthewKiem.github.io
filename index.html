Matthew Kiem - CV

Matthew Kiem is a researcher, educator, designer, and activist based in Sydney, Australia. Matthew is currently a PhD canditate at Western Sydney University. His thesis is titled 'The Coloniality of Design'. Matthew is also an ongoing participant in the xBorder network, and a founding member of the Decolonising Design group.

Qualifications

Bachelor of Design and Bachelor of Art Education (Hons), University of New South Wales

Teaching Experience

Matthew has previously lectured and tutored in design studies at the College of Fine Arts (UNSW Art and Design), the Whitehouse Institute of Design, the University of Technology Sydney, and UTS Insearch.
